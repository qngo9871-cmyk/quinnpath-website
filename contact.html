Enquiries

Contact

Quinn welcomes correspondence from readers, event organisers, and fellow practitioners.

Readers

Questions about the books, responses to what you've read, or thoughts you'd like to share. All genuine reader letters receive a reply in time.

Speaking Enquiries

Quinn is available for talks and conversations on consciousness, contemplative practice, Buddhist scholarship, and the intersection of direct experience with academic study. Please include event details and dates.

Retreat Questions

Questions about intensive retreat — preparation, finding a teacher, the traditions described in Forty-Three Days of Fire, or related matters.

Media & Press

Interview requests, review copies, and press enquiries. Please identify your publication and intended focus.

Quinn reads all correspondence personally. Please allow reasonable time for a response, particularly following retreat periods.

Your details are used only to respond to your enquiry and are not shared with third parties.
